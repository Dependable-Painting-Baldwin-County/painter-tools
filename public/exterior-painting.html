Exterior Painting FAQs

How often should I paint my home's exterior?

How often should I paint my home's exterior?

In our coastal Alabama climate, a high-quality paint job should last 5-7 years. Factors like the type of siding, direct sun exposure, and proximity to saltwater can affect longevity. Regular cleaning helps extend its life.

What is the best time of year to paint outside?

What is the best time of year to paint outside?

Spring and Fall generally offer the best conditions, with moderate temperatures and lower humidity. However, we can paint successfully during many parts of the year by carefully monitoring weather and dew points.

Do you power wash before painting?

Do you power wash before painting?

Yes, absolutely. Power washing is a critical first step to remove dirt, chalking, and mildew. This ensures the new paint has a clean surface to bond to, which is essential for a durable, long-lasting finish.

Can you paint vinyl siding?

Can you paint vinyl siding?

Yes! We use specialized Sherwin-Williams paints (like VinylSafe®) that are formulated to be applied to vinyl siding. This allows you to change your home's color without the high cost of replacement.
