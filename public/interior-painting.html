Interior Painting FAQs

Do I need to move my furniture?

Do I need to move my furniture?

We ask that you move small, fragile, or personal items. Our team will handle moving larger furniture to the center of the room and covering it securely with plastic to ensure it stays protected.

How long will it take to paint a room?

How long will it take to paint a room?

An average-sized room (12'x12') typically takes 1-2 days. This includes prep time, any necessary repairs, two coats of paint, and cleanup. We can provide a more precise timeline with your estimate.

What kind of paint finish should I choose?

What kind of paint finish should I choose?

We recommend a matte or eggshell finish for most walls for its low-sheen, durable quality. For trim and doors, a semi-gloss is ideal for its durability and ease of cleaning. We'll help you choose the perfect finish.

Are the paint fumes harmful?

Are the paint fumes harmful?

We use modern, low-VOC or zero-VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) paints from Sherwin-Williams. This significantly reduces odors and is safer for your family and pets. We also ensure proper ventilation.
